I am an undergraduate researcher at Tufts University working with Professor Elaine Short in The Agent Assistive Behavior and Learning Lab (AABL). My research interest is in Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), specifically in improving accessibility by developing robotic algorithms that encourage trust and communication with users.
